Step 3 - Service times and Visits

___________________________

 

In this window you can insert indirectly the Service Demands (D) by inserting Service times ( S ) and visits ( V )

 

 

Service times (S)

The service time of a customer at station i, Si, is the total amount of time the client spends in service at that station during one visit.

 

 

 

If the model comprise load dependent stations the Load dependent service times / service demands dialog window is shown.

 

 

 

In this window you can set values of service times for each numbers of customers by double-clicking on any buttom in station's row.

 

Double click on LD Settings will open this window:

 

 

 

In this window you can insert the values of the service times for each possible population.

 

 

Visits (V)

The visits of a customer at station i, Vi, is the average number of accesses to the station.
