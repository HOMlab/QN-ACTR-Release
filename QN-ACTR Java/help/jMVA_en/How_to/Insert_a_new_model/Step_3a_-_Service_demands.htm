In this window you can insert directly the Service Demands (D)

 

 

Service demands (D)

 

The service demand of a customer at station i, Di, is the total amount of time the client spends in service at that station during its complete execution.

 

 

 

 

 

If the model comprise load dependent stations the Load dependent service times / service demands dialog window is shown.

 

 

 

In this window you can set values of service times for each numbers of customers by double-clicking on any buttom in station's row.

 

Double click on LD Settings will open this window:

 

 

 

In this window you can insert the values of the service demands for each possible population.
